About Me


I am Carter Cravillion, a passionate front-end web developer with four years of experience in WCTC’s program. I thrive on design, focusing on creating visually compelling and user-friendly digital experiences. As I continue honing my skills, I am dedicated to mastering both aesthetics and functionality, ensuring my work is both engaging and effective.


My Personal Life

Ever since I was young, I’ve had a passion for drawing and design, both on paper and digitally. When I took an introductory web design class in high school, everything suddenly clicked—I was captivated by the way code could transform into shapes, colors, and dynamic visuals on my screen. That moment sparked my curiosity and drive to explore deeper, pushing me to refine my skills and continuously expand my projects.


My Hobbies

Together, these hobbies shape my creativity, critical thinking, and enjoyment of both digital and real-world experiences.
